## Introduction

As EDI moves to require authenticated sign in for access to all applications, the need to include anonymity as a user state for certain individuals (namely, reviewers) is critical. It is not sufficient to assume a users identity will be masked by hidding in plain sight amongst all the other users seeking data in EDI.
